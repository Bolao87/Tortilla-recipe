Spanish Omelette (Tortilla Espa√±ola)

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced potatoes and onions to the pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender but not browned, about 10-15 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Drain the potatoes and onions from the oil and add them to the beaten eggs. Mix well.
  5. Pour the mixture back into the frying pan, spreading it out evenly. Cook over low heat until the eggs are set, about 10-12 minutes.
  6. Carefully flip the omelette using a plate. Slide the omelette back into the pan and cook for another 2-3 minutes until fully cooked.
  7. Transfer the omelette to a serving plate, let it cool slightly, then cut into wedges and serve.
